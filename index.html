About Us

Spreading Light Through Knowledge

In the Name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate

Imamia Online Quran School is not just an online academy — it is a responsibility, a trust, and a mission.

We provide high-quality online Quran education to students around the world. Our goal is to help children and adults learn the Holy Quran with correct Tajweed, proper pronunciation, and beautiful recitation — all from the comfort and safety of their homes.

Our Vision

Our foundation is based on Shia (Fiqh Jafaria) teachings. We aim to spread the light of the Quran with the guidance of Ahlulbayt (A.S.) to homes worldwide. At the same time, we respectfully provide education to Sunni (Fiqh Hanafi) students as well.

We believe in unity, respect, and authentic Islamic learning.

Our Classes

  • Classes are held Monday to Friday (5 days a week)
  • Flexible scheduling available if fewer days are required
  • One-to-one personalized sessions
  • Qualified male and female teachers
  • Students from all countries are welcome

Classes are conducted via Skype. If Skype is not accessible in your area, we also offer WhatsApp as an alternative.
